Vision

ACE LLC is committed to becoming the benchmark for Chinese and foreign educational consulting agencies. It aims to cultivate students and build the best platforms for students to realize their own values and dreams.

Mission

ACE LLC is committed to nurturing talented people who will be fully developed, have a global perspective, be able to embrace the country and the world, and actively work to make the community better.

Our Services

  • China-US Higher Education Institutions International Partnership: We help Chinese universities collaborate with top U.S. institutions to enhance academic programs and attract talented students who may return to contribute to China after graduation.
  • Secondary School Applications and Services in the U.S.: We match students with suitable U.S. schools and provide full support, including visa entry, logistics, academic tracking, integration assistance, and leadership cultivation.
  • Undergraduate and Graduate Degree Programs Applications: We guide students through the application process for bachelor's, master's, and Ph.D. programs in the U.S., Canada, and UK.
  • Talented Students Club: An elite program for students preparing to apply to Top 20 U.S. universities.
  • Academic Competition Club: Includes competitions in math, science, computing, business, and arts.
  • Standardized Test Tutoring and Preparation: Comprehensive training in SAT, ACT, TOEFL, IELTS, AP, and high school subjects.
  • Academic Research: Led by Dr. Zhang, we conduct and publish research on international education in top U.S. academic journals.
  • Parents Career Development in the U.S.: We offer networking and career support for parents looking to expand professionally in the U.S.
  • Public Speaking: Dr. Zhang and Mr. Barrett are available for public speaking at select engagements.
